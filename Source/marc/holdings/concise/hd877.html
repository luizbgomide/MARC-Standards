877 - Item Information-Supplementary Material (R)

MARC 21 Holdings - Concise
2007

Item information about supplementary material that is not separately cataloged in the holdings of the reporting organization. A display may be generated from the contents of field 877 and its related 852 (Location), 854/864 (Captions and Pattern/Enumeration and Chronology), or 867 (Textual Holdings) field to which it is linked by subfield $8 (Field link and sequence number) or related by subfield $3 (Materials specified).

Guidelines for applying the content designators and input conventions defined for field 877 are given in the 876-878 Item Information Fields--General Information section.

Example
877 ##$31986$aACC8761-3-2$jLost$pJ87958763
