FIELD DEFINITION AND SCOPE

Credits for persons or organizations, other than members of the cast, who have participated in the creation and/or production of the work. The introductory term Credits: is usually generated as a display constant.

Field 508 is repeatable to record complex or multiple credit notes.

GUIDELINES FOR APPLYING CONTENT DESIGNATORS

■ INDICATORS

  • Both indicator positions are undefined; each contains a blank (#).

INPUT CONVENTIONS

Punctuation - Field 508 ends with a period unless another mark of punctuation is present.

Display Constant - Introductory term Credits: that often appears with the data recorded in field 508 is not carried in the MARC record. It may be system generated as a display constant associated with the field tag.

CONTENT DESIGNATOR HISTORY

In 2001, the field was changed from Not-repeatable (NR) to Repeatable (R) to record complex or multiple credit notes.
