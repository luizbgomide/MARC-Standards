082 - Dewey Decimal Classification Number (R)

MARC 21 LITE Bibliographic
April 2008

Classification number is taken from Dewey Decimal Classification and Relative Index, the Abridged Dewey Decimal Classification and Relative Index, and electronic updates to either edition. Through 1997, updates where issued in DC& Dewey Decimal Classification Additions, Notes and Decisions.

[Link to MARC 21 Concise Formats]

Indicators
First - Type of edition
0 - Full edition
1 - Abridged edition
Second - Source of classification number
4 - Assigned by agency other than LC
Subfield Codes
$a - Classification number (R)
$b - Item number (NR)
$2 - Edition number (NR)
