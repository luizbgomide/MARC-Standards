653 - Index Term-Uncontrolled (R)

MARC 21 LITE Bibliographic
April 2008

Index term added entry that is not constructed by standard subject heading/thesaurus-building conventions.

[Link to MARC 21 Concise Formats]

Indicators
First - Level of index term
# - No information provided
Second - Type of term or name
# - No information provided
Subfield Code
$a - Uncontrolled term (R)
Index term is from an uncontrolled subject heading system or thesaurus.
Example
653 ##$aMan$aEyes$aDiseases
