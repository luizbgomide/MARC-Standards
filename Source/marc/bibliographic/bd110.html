FIELD DEFINITION AND SCOPE

Corporate name used as a main entry in a bibliographic record.

According to various cataloging rules, main entry under corporate name is assigned to works that represent the collective thought of a body.

Conference and meeting names that are entered subordinately to a corporate body are contained in this field rather than in field 111.

GUIDELINES FOR APPLYING CONTENT DESIGNATORS

Descriptions of the first indicator position and all subfield codes, as well as input conventions for the 110 field, are given in the X10 Corporate Names-General Information section. Because the second indicator is different for various X10 fields, it is not described in the general information section, but is described below.

■ INDICATORS

Second Indicator - Undefined

Undefined and contains a blank (#).
