482 - See From Tracing-Chronological Subdivision (R)

MARC 21 Authority - concise
July 2022

Tracing for a chronological subdivision see from reference.

Used in an established heading or established heading and subdivision record to trace a see from reference from a chronological subdivision not used in an established heading.

See field 182 for descriptions of most content designators.
