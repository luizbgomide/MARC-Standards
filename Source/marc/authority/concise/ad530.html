530 - See Also From Tracing-Uniform Title (R)

MARC 21 Authority - concise
July 2022

Tracing for a uniform title see also from reference.

Used in an established heading or established heading and subdivision record to trace a see also from reference from an established uniform title to a related established heading.

See field 130 for descriptions of most content designators.

Indicators

First - Undefined
# - Undefined
Second - Nonfiling characters
0-9 - Number of nonfiling characters
