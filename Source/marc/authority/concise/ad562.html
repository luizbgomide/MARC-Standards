562 - See Also From Tracing-Medium of Performance Term (R)

MARC 21 Authority - concise
July 2022

Tracing for medium of performance term see also from reference.

Used in an established term record to trace a >see also from reference from an established medium of performance term to a related established term.

See field 162 for descriptions of most content designators.
