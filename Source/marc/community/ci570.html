FIELD DEFINITION AND SCOPE

Note about the persons who administer or are involved in some way with decision-making involving the community information entity.

For instance, the note can include the names of the directors, administrators, advisory groups, boards, chairpersons, etc. The note may contain other information, such as the telephone number and/or the term of office of a particular individual, information pertaining to elections of officers, etc.

GUIDELINES FOR APPLYING CONTENT DESIGNATORS

■ INDICATORS

  • Both indicator positions are undefined; each contains a blank (#).
