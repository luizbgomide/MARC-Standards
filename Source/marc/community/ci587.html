FIELD DEFINITION AND SCOPE

Other information pertaining to the community information entity that is available from the agency identified in subfield $a (Original cataloging agency) of field 040 (Record Source).

GUIDELINES FOR APPLYING CONTENT DESIGNATORS

■ INDICATORS

  • Both indicator positions are undefined; each contains a blank (#).
