FIELD DEFINITION AND SCOPE

Tracing for a named event see also from reference.

Used in an established heading or established heading and subdivision record to trace a see also from reference from an established named event to a related established heading.

GUIDELINES FOR APPLYING CONTENT DESIGNATORS

Most of the content designators defined for field 547 are the same as those defined for field 147 (Heading-Named Event), field 447 (See From Tracing-Named Event), and field 747 (Established Heading Linking Entry-Named Event). Description, guidelines, and input conventions for shared content designators are given in the X47 Named Events-General Information section.

Guidelines for applying the subfield $w (Control subfield), subfield $i (Relationship information), and subfield $4 (Relationship) in field 547 are given in the Tracings and References-General Information section.
