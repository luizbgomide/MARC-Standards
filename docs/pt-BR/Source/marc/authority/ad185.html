FIELD DEFINITION AND SCOPE

Form or genre term used as a heading in a subdivision record.

GUIDELINES FOR APPLYING CONTENT DESIGNATORS

Guidelines for applying the content designators defined for field 185 and input conventions are given in the X85 Form Subdivisions-General Information section.
