521 - Target Audience Note (R)

MARC 21 LITE Bibliographic
April 2008

Information that identifies the specific audience or intellectual level for which the content of the described item is considered appropriate.

[Link to MARC 21 Concise Formats]

Indicators
First - Display constant controller
# - Audience
Second - Undefined
# - Undefined
Subfield Code
$a - Target audience note (R)
Example
521 ##$aProgram designed for geographers, planners, geologists, meteorologists and others who have a professional interest in analyzing spatial data.
