508 - Creation/Production Credits Note (R)

MARC 21 Bibliographic - concise
July 2022

Credits for persons or organizations, other than members of the cast, who have participated in the creation and/or production of the work. The introductory term Credits: is usually generated as a display constant.

Field 508 is repeatable to record complex or multiple credit notes.

Indicators

First - Undefined
# - Undefined
Second - Undefined
# - Undefined

Subfield Codes

$a - Creation/production credits note (NR)
$6 - Linkage (NR)
See description of this subfield in Appendix A: Control Subfields.
$7 - Data provenance (R)
See description of this subfield in Appendix J: Data Provenance Subfields.
$8 - Field link and sequence number (R)
See description of this subfield in Appendix A: Control Subfields.

Examples

508 ##$aProducer, Joseph N. Ermolieff ; director, Lesley Selander ; screenplay, Theodore St. John ; music director, Michel Michelet.
508 ##$aMusic, Michael Fishbein ; camera, George Mo.
508 ##$aFilm editor, Martyn Down ; consultant, Robert F. Miller.
508 ##$aPhotographer, Richard Beymer ; film editor, Charles Pavlich.
