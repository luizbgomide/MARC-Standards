061 - National Library of Medicine Copy Statement (R)

MARC 21 Bibliographic - Concise
2007

Information added to a bibliographic record by the National Library of Medicine that relates to second copies or sets of the described item that are in its collections.

Indicators
First - Undefined
# - Undefined
Second - Undefined
# - Undefined
Subfield Codes
$a - Classification number (R)
$b - Item number (NR)
$c - Copy information (NR)
$8 - Field link and sequence number (R)
See description of this subfield in Appendix A: Control Subfields.
