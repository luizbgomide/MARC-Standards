69X - Local Subject Access Fields (R)

MARC 21 Bibliographic - Full
February 1999

FIELD DEFINITION AND SCOPE

Fields 690-699 are reserved for local subject use and local definition. For interchange purposes, documentation of the structure of the 69X fields and input conventions should be provided to exchange partners by the organization initiating the exchange.
