FIELD DEFINITION AND SCOPE

Scale of a visual material resource, for example a still image or three-dimensional form, given as a textual note.

For cartographic materials, prefer field 255 (Cartographic Mathematical Data) to record the scale information.

GUIDELINES FOR APPLYING CONTENT DESIGNATORS

CONTENT DESIGNATOR HISTORY

Field 507 - Scale Note for Graphic Material [RENAMED, 2022] [REDESCRIBED, 2022]
Historically, field 507 was used for recording scale content of cartographic material. The field was renamed and the definition was broadened to allow for the recording of non-cartographic scale content.
