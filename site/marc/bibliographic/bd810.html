FIELD DEFINITION AND SCOPE

Author/title series added entry in which the author portion is a corporate name.

An 810 field is usually justified by a series statement (field 490) or a general note (field 500) relating to the series. For reproductions, it may be justified by a series statement in subfield $f of field 533 (Reproduction Note).

GUIDELINES FOR APPLYING CONTENT DESIGNATORS

Description of the first indicator position and all subfield codes, as well as input conventions for the 810 field are given in the X10 Corporate Names-General Information section. Because the second indicator is different for various fields, it is not described in the general information section, but is described below.

■ INDICATORS

Second Indicator - Undefined

Undefined and contains a blank (#).
