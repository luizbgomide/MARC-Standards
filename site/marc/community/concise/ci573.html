573 - Credentials Note (R)

MARC 21 Community Information - Concise
2007

Information that the community information entity has been licensed or accredited by a particular agency or organization.

Indicators
First - Undefined
# - Undefined
Second - Undefined
# - Undefined
Subfield Codes
$a - Credentials note (NR)
$6 - Linkage (NR)
See the description of this subfield in Appendix A: Control Subfields.
$8 - Field link and sequence number (R)
See the description of this subfield in Appendix A: Control Subfields.
Examples
573 ##$aCurrent Maine Registered Nurse (RN) license.
573 ##$aPh.D, Political Science--University of Colorado, 1973.
573 ##$aLicensed by the New Jersey State Department of Education.
573 ##$aAccredited by the NYS Board of Regents and the Middle States Association of Colleges and Schools, Commission on Higher Education.
