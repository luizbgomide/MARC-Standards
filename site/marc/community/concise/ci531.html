531 - Eligibility, Fees, Procedures Note (R)

MARC 21 Community Information - Concise
2007

Entity-specific information about such things as who is eligible to use the services, attend the event, join the organization; the fee involved; admission procedures; the waiting period, etc.

Indicators
First - Undefined
# - Undefined
Second - Undefined
# - Undefined
Subfield Codes
$a - Eligibility (NR)
$b - Fee (R)
Can include method of payment information. Meeting room/facility or equipment rental fees can be found in subfield $f (Rental fee) of fields 311 (Meeting Rooms and Facilities Available) or 312 (Equipment Available).
$c - Admission procedures (NR)
$d - Documents required (NR)
$e - Waiting list (NR)
$f - Waiting period (NR)
$6 - Linkage (NR)
See the description of this subfield in Appendix A: Control Subfields.
$8 - Field link and sequence number (R)
See the description of this subfield in Appendix A: Control Subfields.
