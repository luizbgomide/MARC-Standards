245 - Title (NR)

MARC 21 Community Information - Concise
2007

Title of the event, program, etc. being described.

Indicators
First - Undefined
# - Undefined
Second - Nonfiling characters
0 - No nonfiling characters present
1-9 - Number of nonfiling characters present
Subfield Codes
$a - Title (NR)
$b - Remainder of title (NR)
$c - Remainder of field data (NR)
Remaining data in the field that are not more appropriately contained in another subfield.
$h - Medium (NR)
Medium designator used in the title.
$n - Number of part/section of the title (R)
$p - Name of part/section of the title (R)
$6 - Linkage (NR)
See the description of this subfield in Appendix A: Control Subfields.
$8 - Field link and sequence number (R)
See the description of this subfield in Appendix A: Control Subfields.
Examples
245 #0$aAndy Williams Christmas Show.
245 #4$aThe Wonderful Ice Cream Suit.
245 #0$aContemporary Music and Inter-disciplinary Music Theatre.
245 #0$aCity Board of Directors Meeting.
245 #0$aToys for Tots.
