570 - Personnel Note (R)

MARC 21 Community Information - Concise
2007

Note about the persons who administer or are involved in some way with decision-making involving the community information entity.

Indicators
First - Undefined
# - Undefined
Second - Undefined
# - Undefined
Subfield Codes
$a - Personnel note (NR)
May include the telephone number and/or the term of office of a particular individual.
$b - Election information (NR)
Includes information such as when the election of officers takes place or when officials in the organization are rotated.
$6 - Linkage (NR)
See the description of this subfield in Appendix A: Control Subfields.
$8 - Field link and sequence number (R)
See the description of this subfield in Appendix A: Control Subfields.
