Appendix B - Record Examples

MARC 21 Community Information
October 2001

Examples in this appendix reflect the application of MARC content designators in community information records. Although the data is taken from actual records, these records are included for illustrative purposes only and are thus not usable for any other purposes. The types of examples are:

  • Agency
  • Organization
  • Program
  • Event
  • Individual
