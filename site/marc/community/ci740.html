FIELD DEFINITION AND SCOPE

Added entry for a title of a specific program within the entity. Such titles may be cited in field 505 (Programs Note). Variant titles of the program are contained in field 246 (Varying Form of Title).

The field is limited to titles of programs within the community information entity.

GUIDELINES FOR APPLYING CONTENT DESIGNATORS

■ INDICATORS

First Indicator - Nonfiling characters
Value that specifies the number of character positions associated with a definite or indefinite article (e.g., Le, An) at the beginning of a program title that are disregarded in sorting and filing processes.
0 - No nonfiling characters
No initial article character positions are disregarded.Diacritical marks or special characters at the beginning of a program title field that does not begin with an initial article are not counted as nonfiling characters.An initial definite or indefinite article may also simply be deleted in the formulation of the program title field.If the initial article is retained but is not to be disregarded in sorting and filing processes, value 0 is used.
1-9 - Number of nonfiling characters present
Title begins with a definite or indefinite article that is disregarded in sorting and filing processes. Any diacritical mark, space or mark of punctuation associated with the article and any space or mark of punctuation preceding the first filing character after the article is included in the count of nonfiling characters. Any diacritic, however, associated with the first filing character is not included in the count of nonfiling characters.
Second Indicator - Undefined
Undefined and contains a blank (#).

■ SUBFIELD CODES

$a - Title
Specific program title, exclusive of any name or number of a part/section.
505 ##$aVISTA - Help low income poverty persons -- RSVP - Enable senior citizens to volunteer their time to community services -- Foster Grandparent Program -- Senior Companions.
740 0#$aVISTA.
740 0#$aRSVP.
740 0#$aFoster Grandparent Program.
740 0#$aSenior Companions.
$n - Number of part/section
Number designation for a part/section of a program title. Numbering is defined as an indication of sequencing in any form, e.g., Part 1, Section A.
$p - Name of part/section
Name designation of a part/section of a program title.
$6 - Linkage
See the description of this subfield in Appendix A: Control Subfields.
$8 - Field link and sequence number
See the description of this subfield in Appendix A: Control Subfields.

INPUT CONVENTIONS

Punctuation - Field 740 ends with a mark of punctuation or a closing parenthesis. A mark of punctuation is placed inside a closing quotation mark.
Spacing - No spaces are used in initialisms. One space is used between preceding and succeeding initials if an abbreviation consists of more than a single phrase (e.g., W. Va.).
Initial Articles - Initial articles (e.g., The) occurring at the beginning of specific program title fields are usually omitted in most languages (except when the intent is to file on the article). Any diacritics and/or special characters occurring at the beginning of the field are retained. Note that such characters are usually ignored for purposes of sorting and filing.
Display Constant - Arabic or roman numbers and the word Title: which precede added entry fields on some displays are not carried in the MARC record. These may be system generated as display constants associated with the field tag.
