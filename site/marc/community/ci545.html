FIELD DEFINITION AND SCOPE

Biographical or historical information about an individual, an institution or other entity present in the 1XX primary name field or historical information about the event, program, etc. named in field 245 (Title).

GUIDELINES FOR APPLYING CONTENT DESIGNATORS

■ INDICATORS

  • Both indicator positions are undefined; each contains a blank (#).
