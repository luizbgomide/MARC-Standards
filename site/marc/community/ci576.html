FIELD DEFINITION AND SCOPE

Information pertaining to services available, such as child care arrangements, speakers bureaus, or mutual support groups.

GUIDELINES FOR APPLYING CONTENT DESIGNATORS

■ INDICATORS

First Indicator - Type of information
Whether the data in the field pertain to child care arrangements, speakers bureau, or mutual support group information.
0 - Child care arrangements
This information in coded form may be contained in field 008/08 (Fixed-Length Data Elements/Child care arrangements).
576 0#$aChild care available during church services.
1 - Speakers bureau
This information in coded form may be contained in field 008/09 (Speakers bureau).
576 1#$aSpeakers available.
2 - Mutual support groups
This information in coded form may be contained in field 008/10 (Mutual support groups).
576 2#$aSupport group for those ages 21+; family and friends welcome.
Second Indicator - Undefined
Undefined and contains a blank (#).

■ SUBFIELD CODES

$a - Services available note
Text of the note.
576 0#$aDay care provided for children of registered full and part-time students.
576 1#$aWill provide speaker to interested groups.
576 1#$aSpeakers available. Offers a "Quiet Please Program" on care and treatment of hearing, directed toward high school age and elderly.
576 2#$aSupport group available; meets monthly, first Sat., 10 a.m.
576 2#$aSelf-help program includes step-by-step quitting techniques and lifetime support in staying cigarette-free; group led by trained ex-smoker.
$6 - Linkage
See the description of this subfield in Appendix A: Control Subfields.
$8 - Field link and sequence number
See the description of this subfield in Appendix A: Control Subfields.

INPUT CONVENTIONS

Punctuation - Field 576 ends with a period unless another mark of punctuation is present.
