865 - Enumeration and Chronology-Indexes (R)

MARC 21 Holdings - Concise
2007

Enumeration and chronology of indexes that are not separately cataloged in the holdings of the reporting organization. A display may be generated from the contents of field 865 and the related field 855 (Captions and Pattern--Indexes) to which it is linked by subfield $8 (Field link and sequence number).

Guidelines for applying the content designators defined for field 865 are given in the 863-865 Enumeration and Chronology Fields--General Information section.

Examples
865 4#$81.1$a1969/1978$oTen year cumulative index
865 4#$81.1$a1918-1921$oIndex$zbound at end of volume to which it applies
