868 - Textual Holdings-Indexes (R)

MARC 21 Holdings - Concise
2007

Textual description of the holdings of indexes in the collections of the reporting organization. It may be used instead of or in addition to field 865 (Enumeration and Chronology--Indexes) and any related 855 (Captions and Pattern--Indexes) to record and/or display all or part of the holdings.

Guidelines for applying the content designators defined for field 868 and input conventions are given in the 866-868 Textual Holdings-General Information section.

Example
868 40$80$a1937-1942, 1946-1968, plus 1969/1978 cumulative vol.
