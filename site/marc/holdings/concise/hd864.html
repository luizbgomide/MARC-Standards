864 - Enumeration and Chronology-Supplementary Material (R)

MARC 21 Holdings - Concise
2007

Enumeration and chronology of supplementary material that are not separately cataloged in the holdings of the reporting organization. A display may be generated from the contents of field 864 and the related field 854 (Captions and Pattern--Supplementary Material) to which it is linked by subfield $8 (Field link and sequence number).

Guidelines for applying the content designators defined for field 864 are given in the 863-865 Enumeration and Chronology Fields--General Information section.

Examples
864 30$81.1$a1910-1988
864 40$81.1$a1-3$i1983-1985
864 30$83.1$a1-3$i1983-1985$xbind in 1 v. when next issue arrives
864 ##$zsuppl.
