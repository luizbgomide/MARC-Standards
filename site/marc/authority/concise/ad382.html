382 - Medium of Performance (R)

MARC 21 Authority - concise
July 2022

The instrumental, vocal, and/or other medium of performance for which a musical work was originally conceived or for which a musical expression is written or performed. May be used to differentiate a musical work or expression from another with the same title.

Indicators

First - Display constant controller
# - No information provided
0 - Medium of performance
1 - Partial medium of performance
2 - Medium of performance of musical content of representative expression
3 - Partial medium of performance of musical content of representative expression
Second - Undefined
# - Undefined
