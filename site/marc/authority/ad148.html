FIELD DEFINITION AND SCOPE

In an established heading record, field 148 contains the established form of a chronological term. In records for references, this field contains an unestablished form of a chronological term.

GUIDELINES FOR APPLYING CONTENT DESIGNATORS

Guidelines for applying the content designators defined for field 148 and input conventions are given in the X48 Chronological Terms-General Information section.
