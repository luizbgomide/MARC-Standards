FIELD DEFINITION AND SCOPE

In an established heading record, field 147 contains the established form of a named event. In records for references, this field contains an unestablished form of a named event.

GUIDELINES FOR APPLYING CONTENT DESIGNATORS

Guidelines for applying the content designators defined for field 147 and input conventions are given in the X47 Named Events-General Information section.
