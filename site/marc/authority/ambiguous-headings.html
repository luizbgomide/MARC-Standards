Appendix D - Alphabetical List of Ambiguous Headings

MARC 21 Authority
October 2002
Listing of categories of heading entities with an indication of the field type and tag for each. Categories marked X00, X10, X11, and X30 belong to the field types personal name, corporate name, conference name and title, respectively. Categories marked X50 and X51 belong to the field types topical terms and geographic names.
This list also appears in both the MARC 21 Format for Bibliographic Data and the MARC 21 Format for Community Information.
