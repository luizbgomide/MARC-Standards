FIELD DEFINITION AND SCOPE

Tracing for a named event see from reference.

Used in an established heading or established heading and subdivision record to trace a see from reference from a named event not used in an established heading.

GUIDELINES FOR APPLYING CONTENT DESIGNATORS

Most of the content designators defined for field 447 are the same as those defined for field 147 (Heading-Named Event), field 547 (See Also From Tracing-Named Event), and field 747 (Established Heading Linking Entry-Named Event). Description, guidelines, and input conventions for shared content designators are given in the X47 Named Events-General Information section.

Guidelines for using subfield $w (Control subfield), subfield $i (Relationship information), and subfield $4 (Relationship) in field 447 are given in the Tracings and References-General Information section.
