FIELD DEFINITION AND SCOPE

Topical, form, or language term used as a heading in a subdivision record.

GUIDELINES FOR APPLYING CONTENT DESIGNATORS

Guidelines for applying the content designators defined for field 180 and input conventions are given in the X80 General Subdivisions-General Information section.
