DEFINITION AND SCOPE

Fields 640-646 contain information concerning the treatment of a 1XX series heading when that heading is used in bibliographic records. Other series treatment information is contained in the fixed-length data elements of field 008 and in link, standard number, and call number fields in the 01X-09X fields.

CONTENT DESIGNATOR HISTORY

Indicator 1 - Numbering example
0 - Current numbering example [OBSOLETE, 1997] [CAN/MARC only]
1 - Earlier numbering example [OBSOLETE, 1997] [CAN/MARC only]
