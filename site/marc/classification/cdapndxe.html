FORMAT CHANGE LIST

Update No. 37, December 2023
There are no changes to the MARC 21 Format for Classification Data for Update No. 37 (December 2023) resulting from proposals that were considered by the MARC 21 community since the publication of Update No. 36 (June 2023).
