855 - Captions and Pattern-Indexes (R)

MARC 21 Holdings - Concise
2007

Caption that is appropriate for each level of enumeration and chronology that is present in a related 865 Enumeration and Chronology Indexes field and codes that define the publication pattern for the item. A display may be generated from the contents of field 855 and the related 865 field(s) to which it is linked by subfield $8 (Field link and sequence number).

Guidelines for applying the content designators defined for field 855 are given in the Caption and Pattern Fields--General Information section.

Examples

855 ##$81$a(year)$oten year cumulation
