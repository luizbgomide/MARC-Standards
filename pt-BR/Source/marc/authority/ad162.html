FIELD DEFINITION AND SCOPE

Medium of performance term used as a heading in an established term record or a traced or an untraced reference record. The term may consist of more than one word.

In an established term record, field 162 contains the established form of a medium of performance term. In records for references, this field contains an unestablished form of a medium of performance term.

GUIDELINES FOR APPLYING CONTENT DESIGNATORS

Guidelines for applying the content designators defined for field 162 and input conventions are given in the X62 Medium of Performance Terms-General Information section.
