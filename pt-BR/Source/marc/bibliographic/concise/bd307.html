307 - Hours, Etc. (R)

MARC 21 Bibliographic - Concise
2007

Chronological information identifying the days and/or times an item is available or accessible. Used primarily in records for electronic resources.

Indicators
First - Display constant controller
# - Hours
8 - No display constant generated
Second - Undefined
# - Undefined
Subfield Codes
$a - Hours (NR)
$b - Additional information (NR)
$6 - Linkage (NR)
See description of this subfield in Appendix A: Control Subfields.
$8 - Field link and sequence number (R)
See description of this subfield in Appendix A: Control Subfields.
Examples
307 ##$aM-F, 9:30am-3:30pm, USA EST.
307 8#$aDate: Dec. 1, 1993, 2:00 p.m.
307 ##$aM-F, 6:30am-9:00pm (EST);$bwith brief interruptions for periodic update/backup of data.
307 ##$aDaily, 7am-7pm;$btext files only.
