037 - Source of Acquisition (R)

MARC 21 Bibliographic - concise
November 2016

Source of acquisition information for the item or its reproduction.

Indicators

First - Source of acquisition sequence
# - Not applicable/No information provided/Earliest
2 - Intervening
3 - Current/Latest
Second - Undefined
# - Undefined

Subfield Codes

$a - Stock number (NR)
Numbers such as distributor, publisher, or vendor numbers for resources other than music, music-related, or audiovisual materials are also recorded in this subfield.
$b - Source of stock number/acquisition (NR)
$c - Terms of availability (R)
$f - Form of issue (R)
$g - Additional format characteristics (R)
$n - Note (R)
$3 - Materials specified (NR)
$5 - Institution to which field applies (R)
See description of this subfield in Appendix A: Control Subfields.
$6 - Linkage (NR)
See description of this subfield in Appendix A: Control Subfields.
$8 - Field link and sequence number (R)
See description of this subfield in Appendix A: Control Subfields.
