FIELD DEFINITION AND SCOPE

Information about the current and former issuing bodies of a continuing resource.

Includes notes containing editing, compiling, or translating information that involves an issuing body and notes denoting the item as an official organ of a society, etc.

Field 550 is generally used for issuing bodies traced in a 7XX added entry field. The field explains their relationship to the publication and justifies the 7XX added entries. Notes relating to commercial publishers, distributors, or places of publication are recorded in field 500 (General Note).
