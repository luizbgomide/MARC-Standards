DEFINITION AND SCOPE

Subject access entries and terms. Most of these fields contain subject added entries or access terms based on the lists and authority files identified in the second indicator (Thesaurus) or in subfield ‡2 (Source of heading or term). One field contains uncontrolled subject access data.

Descriptions of the first indicator and all subfield codes, as well as input conventions for the 600, 610, and 611 fields, are given in the following General Information sections: X00, X10, and X11. The second indicator is described in the specific section for each field. All content designators for the 630-658 fields are described in the specific section for each field.
